## Thesis

Modern real-time light transport can be viewed as the maintenance of small, weighted, transported empirical measures over a screen-time bundle of path-space fibers. Reuse moves those measures across domains; current shading queries test them; Jacobians correct density under deformation; residuals reveal unexplained evidence; and curvature reveals where different reuse paths stop agreeing.
