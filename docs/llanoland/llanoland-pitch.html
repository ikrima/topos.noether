Easter Eggs for Those Who Know

THE SPREAD
Speed-reading arguments at 300+ words per minute. Multi-hit attack. Just like those chaotic 1AC constructives from 1998-2002.
EVIDENCE TUBS
Colored plastic containers organized by category: Inherency, Harms, Solvency. The sacred tools of every serious CX debater.
THE FLOW CHART
Track every argument across pages. Miss one dropped argument = instant loss. "If you drop it, you lose it."
JUDGE PARADIGMS
Stock Issues, Tabula Rasa, Policy Maker, Kritik Judge... and the nightmare scenario: Lay Judge (spread too fast = auto-loss).
