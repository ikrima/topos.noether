String(3) Cocycle Explorer

A live computation of David Roberts' explicit cocycle for the nontrivial String(3)-bundle on S⁵. All formulas from arXiv:2203.04544, evaluated in the browser — nothing precomputed.

Parameter space

You are driving five real dimensions: the four components of q ∈ ℍ× (a point in the overlap of the two charts of S⁴ = ℍℙ¹), and the path parameter s ∈ [0,1] (which slides you along the path in Spin(3)).

Views

wings — the ruled surface swept out by T± as q traces a loop in ℍ×. What Roberts originally plotted in 2014.

pair — the single pair of paths T+(q) and T−(q) for the current q, each starting at the identity and meeting at a common endpoint. The s-slider animates a bead along both.

fiber — fix s, sweep q over a 2-sphere in ℍ; the image is a 2-dimensional sheet in Spin(3) — a slice of the lift at a single instant.

difference — the based loop TΩ(q) = T−(q)·T+(q)⁻¹ in Spin(3). This is what lives on the triple overlap, and it is the object whose central lift carries the cocycle's essential information.
